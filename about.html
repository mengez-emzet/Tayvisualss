Our Story

We don't take photos — we make memories.

Born from a love of light, shadow, and the stories in between.

TayVisualss was founded on a simple belief: that every person, brand, and moment has a visual story worth telling with intention. What began as a passion for portraiture has grown into a full-spectrum creative studio serving clients across Africa and beyond.

We are not chasing trends. We are building a visual language — one that is timeless, bold, and unmistakably ours. Our work spans photography, videography, creative direction, and brand content, all unified by a commitment to craft over convenience.

When you work with TayVisualss, you're not hiring a photographer. You're gaining a creative partner who is as invested in your story as you are.
