Privacy Policy.

Last updated: January 2026

1. Who We Are

TayVisualss is a photography and videography studio based in Gweru, Zimbabwe. We operate the website at tayvisualss.com. When this policy says "we", "us" or "our", it means TayVisualss. When it says "you", it means you, the visitor or client.

For questions about this policy, contact us at: hello@tayvisualss.com

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect data you actively provide to us. This includes:

  • Contact form: First name, last name, email address, service interest, and your message.
  • Booking form: First name, last name, email, phone number, preferred service, preferred date and time, shoot location, project description, and how you found us.
  • Communications: Any emails or messages you send us directly.

We do not collect payment card details through this website. Payments are handled separately in person or via third-party processors.

We do not currently use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels on this site.

3. Why We Collect It

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry or booking request
  • To schedule and confirm shoot sessions
  • To send you project-related communications (quotes, invoices, delivery links)
  • To improve how we handle future client enquiries

We will never use your data for unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

4. How We Store Your Data

Form submissions are received via email to our secure business inbox. We do not store form data in a public database. Client records are kept in private, password-protected storage.

We retain client communication records for a maximum of 3 years after your last interaction with us, after which they are permanently deleted.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@tayvisualss.com.

5. Who We Share It With

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to any third party. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With subcontractors (e.g. second photographers or editors) strictly for fulfilling your project, under confidentiality
  • If required by law or a valid legal order from a Zimbabwean authority

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Objection — object to us processing your data for a specific purpose

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@tayvisualss.com. We will respond within 7 business days.

7. Image & Content Rights

All photographs and videos on this website are the intellectual property of TayVisualss. Unauthorised reproduction, download, or commercial use of any content from this site is strictly prohibited and may constitute copyright infringement.

If you are a past client and wish to use images from your session for personal or commercial purposes, please refer to your client agreement or contact us to confirm the scope of your usage rights.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms such as YouTube and Instagram. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures on this website including HTTPS encryption, security headers, and protection against common web vulnerabilities. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hello@tayvisualss.com
